Regular expression 
****************** 

  What is Regex?
  ************** 
    Definition 1: 
      A sequence of symbols and characters expressing a string or pattern to be searched from within a longer piece of text.

    Definition 2: 
      A regular expression (regex) defines a search pattern for strings. The search pattern can be anything from a simple character, a fixed string or a complex expression containing special characters describing the pattern.
    Definition 3:
        Regular Expressions (regex or regexp) are powerful pattern-matching tools that allow you to search, manipulate, and validate text with incredible precision. They consist of a sequence of characters that define a search pattern, primarily used for string matching operations.
